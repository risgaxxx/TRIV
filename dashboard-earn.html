Important Details About ETH2


1
ETH 2.0 vs ETH
ETH2 funds, including staked ETH and ETH2 rewards, will be inaccessible until an unknown date in the future when transfers are enabled.
2
Deposit
Depositing ETH1 to the ETH2 beacon chain is irrevocable.
3
Funds and Rewards
ETH2 funds, including staked ETH and ETH2 rewards, will be inaccessible until an unknown date in the future when transfers are enabled.
4
Staking and validation
Staking involves slashing risks and funds can be lost if Staked fails to perform as a validator or acts maliciously.
5
Unstake
Staked ETH 2.0 cannot be unstaked or transferred on the Ethereum network until 2022. This means that clients should only stake ETH 2.0 that they plan to hold long-term.
